- `<em>` <small>語義上的強調/注重</small>
- `<i>` <small>視覺上的斜體</small>
- `<u>` <small></small>
- `<strong>` <small>語義上的強調</small>
- `<small>` <small>縮小字體</small>

- `<sup>` <small>上標字</small>
- `<ins>` <small>插入字</small>
- `<s>` <small>刪除字</small>
- `<del>` <small>刪除字</small>
